  • A Light-hearted Spring & Summer

    For this collection, Decoster sent 40 people from all walks of life down the runway that embody what the brand stands for : an easy - going , casual, and comfortable lifestyle. They were not the usual skinny and glamorous models you would normally find at fashion week ; but rather just regular people. There were grandmothers , students, artists, mothers & daughters, identical twins, and just about anyone that you would meet in your everyday life. People come from all sorts of places, do all sorts of things , and come in all sorts of shapes & sizes; and it's Decoster's goal to celebrate this.
